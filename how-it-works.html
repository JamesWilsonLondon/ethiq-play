Process

What working with Ethiq feels like

The short version is simple. Clear scope, small shortlist, direct feedback. Below is what that looks like for both sides.

For teams

  • 1. Scope · We talk through your team, product and constraints, not just a job spec.
  • 2. Plan · We agree on profile, salary band, timeline and interview steps.
  • 3. Search · We run a focused search and keep you updated once or twice a week.
  • 4. Shortlist · You see a small group of candidates who match what you asked for.
  • 5. Offers · We help you run a fair process and close the right person.

For engineers

  • 1. Context · You tell us what you want and what you avoid.
  • 2. Options · We send a short list of roles that match your bar.
  • 3. Process · We explain how each company hires so there are no surprises.
  • 4. Feedback · You get honest notes from both sides while you talk.
  • 5. Decision · We help you compare offers and make a calm choice.

How we use automation

We use simple tools to keep the search organised and fast, not to replace humans.

  • Structured notes on every search and every interview
  • Clear pipelines so nothing slips through gaps
  • Light touch use of AI to draft outreach and notes, always checked by us

What we will not do

  • We do not spam your inbox or your candidates.
  • We do not send you CVs that do not match the brief.
  • We do not hide salary ranges or push you into roles that are not right.

Privacy at Ethiq

We work with people, not just data. This page is a short overview of how we handle information from candidates and clients. It is not legal advice and we will keep improving it as Ethiq grows.

What we collect

  • Contact details that you share with us, such as your name and email.
  • CVs, profiles and notes that relate to your search for a role or a hire.
  • Basic usage data from our site, such as page views in an anonymous form.

Why we collect it

  • To help you find a role or hire that matches what you asked for.
  • To keep track of searches so we do not waste your time.
  • To run Ethiq as a small, stable business.

How to reach us

If you want to update, export or delete your data with us, email hello@ethiq.io and we will respond as soon as we can.

Email hello@ethiq.io if you have any questions about privacy at Ethiq.

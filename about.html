Ethiq is a small team that cares about the long term

We started Ethiq after many years of helping start ups and scale ups hire engineers, data people and the leaders around them. The short goal is better hires with less drama for everyone involved.

How we think about hiring

Good hiring is simple but not easy. Clear roles, real conversations and a fair process will beat clever tricks most of the time.

We try to be the partner who keeps you honest on things like salary, scope and interview flow. That helps you ship product and helps engineers feel respected.

We work mainly with early and growth stage companies who care more about strong builders than huge hiring funnels.
